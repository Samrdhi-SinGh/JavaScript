Numbers & Maths :-

Maths can be done on JavaScript.

Operation Precedence (or Order of operations) :-

a. () : Brackets have the highest precedence.
b. *,/: Left to Right precedence.
c. +,-: Left to Right precedence.

Percent :-
1% = 1/100
     Per/Cent

Terminologies :-
a. Integer: 2,3,4
b. Floating point numbers Or Float: 2.2,3.4

Float :-
Float when calculated sometimes gives inaccuracy like it comes near the exact answer. 
Like; 20.95 + 7.99 = 28.9399999999--98.
This is inaccurate.

When working with money in Dollars ($):
To not get into problems, 
a. Always calculate in 'Cents'.
b. Then covert it into 'Dollar' by dividing by 100.
Like, 10.95 + 2.75
= 1095 + 275 = 1370 / 100 = 13.70

How to round a number in Js :-
Rounding means taking a decimal or float and making it to the nearest integer.
To Round a number: "Math.round(Number)"
Example: 2.2 = Math.round(2.2) = 2
2.7 = Math.round(2.7) = 3

To calculate a dollar & round it:
